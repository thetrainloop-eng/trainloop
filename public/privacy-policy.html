Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2025

1. Introduction

TrainLoop ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Google account information (via OAuth 2.0 authentication)
  • Google Drive files and folder information that you authorize us to access
  • Document content and metadata for ingestion and analysis purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your account and access your Google Drive files
  • To ingest and process documents from your selected Google Drive folders
  • To extract text content and compute change hashes for document tracking
  • To maintain immutable change records for compliance and audit purposes

4. Google Drive Access

TrainLoop requests read-only access to your Google Drive. We:

  • Only access files and folders that you explicitly authorize
  • Never modify, delete, or share your files
  • Never access files outside the folders you specify
  • Store file metadata and content hashes locally for change tracking

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally in our SQLite database, including:

  • Document metadata and versions
  • Content hashes (SHA256)
  • Change records and ingestion run history

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including:

  • OAuth 2.0 authentication for secure Google account integration
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure storage of credentials and tokens

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information or Google Drive data with third parties, except:

  • As required by law or government request
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your Google Drive at any time through your Google Account settings

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through your Replit project.
